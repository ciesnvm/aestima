Engineered for
the Infinite

Every system aboard the Aestima vessel is designed for sustained deep-space operation. From propulsion to life support, we have reimagined what it means to travel between worlds.

Continuous acceleration to 0.1c using xenon-fueled electric thrusters. Silent, efficient, relentless.
